seQRets Recover
The lifeboat · Works offline · One file forever

Let's get your secret back.

If you have the shares and the password, this page will recover your secret. Nothing you enter here leaves your device.

For extra safety: turn off Wi-Fi and unplug any Ethernet cable before entering your password. Then open this page in a private / incognito browser window with extensions disabled. That way nothing can leave your device and nothing is saved or readable by other software.

1

Add shares or an inheritance plan

Drop in QR images from your Qards, share text files, or an encrypted inheritance-plan JSON file. We'll figure out which one you have. Shares look like seQRets|…|sha256:…; plans are JSON with salt and data fields.

Drop files here or click to browse

Camera scanning is unavailable when this page is opened directly from a file. For the live-scan experience, open the hosted version at seqrets.github.io/seQRets-Recover. You can still upload or paste your share below.

Or paste share text

On your phone, open the camera app and point it at the QR on your Qard. When the text appears above the QR, tap it, choose Copy, then paste it here.
